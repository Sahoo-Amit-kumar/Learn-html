ODISHA

Some Interesting facts about Odisha

  • Home of the Jagannath Temple & Rath Yatra – Odisha is famous for the Jagannath Temple in Puri, where the world’s largest chariot festival, Rath Yatra, is held every year, attracting millions of devotees.
  • Konark Sun Temple – A UNESCO World Heritage Site – Built in the 13th century, this temple is shaped like a gigantic chariot with intricately carved stone wheels and horses, dedicated to the Sun God.
  • Largest Brackish Water Lagoon in Asia – Chilika Lake, spread over 1,100 sq km, is Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon and a haven for migratory birds, including flamingos.
  • Birthplace of Classical Dance Odissi – Odisha is the origin of Odissi dance, one of the oldest classical dance forms of India, known for its grace, sculpturesque poses, and devotional themes.
  • Rich in Tribal Culture & Handicrafts – Odisha is home to more than 60 tribal communities, each with unique traditions, art, and music. The state is also famous for handicrafts like Pattachitra paintings, silver filigree (Tarakasi), and appliqué work of Pipili.
